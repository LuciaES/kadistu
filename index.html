Wood Therapy 

Wood therapy originated in Asia but was adapted heavily in some South American countries. Wood therapy is known for being a sculpting tool but can also improve your overall health. I use wood therapy to assist with body improvement both in the health and beauty department.

Wood therapy helps to break down fat cells, eliminate toxins, improve blood circulation, and kick the lymphatic system into action.  

Cupping
